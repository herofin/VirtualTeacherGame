  • Font size
    • An approximate 14-point heading should be used for the document’s title area.
    • The rest of the document should be around 11-12 point font, though diversions from this are fine if it improves readability.
  • Spacing
    • 1-2 empty lines between headings (use best judgment)
    • 0-1 empty lines between paragraphs (use best judgment)
  • Spelling & Grammar
    • Documents should be proofread by at least one other teammate who did not author the document.
    • When writing numbers, prefer the digit representation and not the word representation. For example, write 42 and not forty-two.
  • Beginning of a Document
    • All documents must include the following three lines at the very top (formatted as they are in this document):
      • COP4331C: Processes for Object Oriented Software Development
      • Virtual Teacher/Mentor
      • <title of document>
    • All documents must next include a modification history in the form of a table. (See this document's modification history for formatting)
    • All documents must then include the team name and the names/emails of the team members immediately following the document’s title. (See this document's team information for formatting)
  • Table of Contents
    • All documents must include a table of contents, where each entry is a link to the specific area of the page. (unless the document is very brief)
    • The entries must be presented in the table of contents in the same order they appear in the document.
