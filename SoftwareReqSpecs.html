SECTION 1: Introduction

Software to be Produced:

     This program is designed to serve as an educational facility for a user between the ages of 5 and 15. It will teach the user as a student a variety of subjects in math, history, language arts, and science using a simulated school environment. The game will progress through a school year where the student will learn subjects of mathematics, science, language arts, and history starting from an elementary level and finishing at an entry-level college education.

SECTION 2: Product Overview

Assumptions:

  • The user will have a standard computer in this era.
  • User may need upwards of 2 gigs of RAM, at least a duo core processor, a dedicated graphics card (low-end), Windows XP, Vista or 7 operating system.
  • The program should stand alone and not require other systems or external processes.

Stakeholders:

  • Parents who want to see their children get outside education
  • Children that want extra help with school
  • Tutors that can employ the software for their students
  • Schools that want to see an overall improvement in their students’ academics
